FAQ

How does this work?

Click Start when you're ready to start your drive.
The camera keeps the last 5 minutes of recording.
When something happens, press Save Video.

Why do you need camera access?

To function as a dash cam, camera access is required.

Do you store any videos or personal information?

No. Everything stays on your own device.

Will it work with the screen off?

No.

About RideCam.ca

Ridecam.ca lets you use your phone as a Dash Camera to capture important events while driving.

Ridecam.ca is 100% free and collects no personal data.
All video stays only on your device.
